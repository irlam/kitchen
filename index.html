Tips

For 2D Floorplan:

  • To Draw walls, select the button and click to at starting of point to draw and reclick at the endpoint. Press ESC to stop drawing walls.
  • To Delete walls, select the button and simply click on the wall, once highlighted in red
  • To Move walls, select the button and click and drag from a specific wall to extend it

For 3D Render:

  • To change wall/floor texture, click on the wall and floor that you would like to change and pick a texture from the dropdown box in the sub-menu
  • Use mouse, scroll wheel and arrow keys to operate the camera. To reset the camera click the '3D Render' button below
  • To add items, click the button and select an item from the catalogue

To Reset your floorplan, select the "New Layout" button
